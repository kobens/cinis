Cinis Recycling is the markets first recycler of different ash types without any landfilling.

The company was founded in 2010 and is located in the Northern Jutlandic town of Arden, Denmark.

We have developed and run a high-technology facility producing potassium fertilizers out of fly ash from biomass power plants.
